# PostPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

PostPilot ("we", "Bit Skyber") is a social media auto-posting application.

## Data We Collect

- Account information from Clerk (email, name) when you sign in
- LinkedIn OAuth tokens and profile information when you connect your account
- Posts and content you create in the app

## How We Use Data

- To authenticate you and connect your social media accounts
- To publish posts on your behalf when you request it
- We do not sell your personal data

## Data Storage

- Data is stored securely in our database (PostgreSQL)
- OAuth tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to the browser

## Third Parties

- Clerk (authentication)
- LinkedIn (OAuth and posting)
- Neon (database hosting)
- Vercel (app hosting)
